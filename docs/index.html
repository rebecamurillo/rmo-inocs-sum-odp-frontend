Monitoring measures around New Shared Mobility

From pre-implementation surveys to real-world impact analysis, here's how data flows through the SUM project.

    1. Before
      Baseline Survey
      We start by collecting baseline mobility KPIs in each Living Lab before any interventions are applied. This includes modal share, user preferences, and transport accessibility data.

      Output: üìä Intervention logs, updated KPIs
    3. After

Measures for Seamless Shared Urban Mobility

Measures are policy actions, infrastructure changes, service rollouts, or regulatory shifts implemented by cities to impact how people move through urban spaces. Each measure falls under one of two strategic categories:

üî¥ Push measures

Push measures are restrictions designed to discourage private car use and reduce car dominance in urban environments.

Congestion Charges
Parking Charges
Restricted Parking
Limited Traffic Zone / Pedestrianisation
Parking Supply Management
Speed Limits
